Software

RepositoryAdvantages
Helmholtz Codebase
  • Gitlab instance available for anyone with a Helmholtz Center Login.
GitLab
  • Built-in CI/CD pipelines.
  • Comprehensive project management.
  • Supports self-hosting.
GitHub
  • Largest developer community.
  • Easy collaboration.
  • Integration with Zenodo for DOIs.
Zenodo
  • Free archival with DOI assignment.
  • Integrates with GitHub.
  • Open access to datasets and software.
SourceForge
  • Established platform for open-source.
  • Project management tools.
  • Wide visibility for research projects.

Image Datasets Across Domains

RepositoryAdvantages
InfiniteSpace / dCache
  • Unlimited storage for projects within Helmholtz.
  • Access through Helmholtz ID.
  • Data HTTP - streamable.
Kaggle Datasets
  • Diverse datasets across disciplines.
  • Active community and competitions.
  • User-friendly tools.
Open Science Framework (OSF)
  • Multidisciplinary support.
  • Collaboration and project management.
  • Promotes open access.
Open Images Dataset
  • Millions of annotated images.
  • Rich annotations (bounding boxes, segmentation).
  • Regular updates.
Figshare
  • Supports various data types.
  • Easy sharing with privacy options.
  • DOI for citation.
Zenodo
  • Free and open access.
  • GitHub integration.
  • Assigns DOIs for datasets.
FAIRsharing
  • Curated registry of standards, databases, and data policies.
  • Supports FAIR data principles.
  • Helps researchers find appropriate data resources.
re3data
  • Comprehensive registry of research data repositories.
  • Covers various disciplines and repository types.
  • Provides standardized metadata for repository evaluation.

Image Datasets in Health

RepositoryAdvantages

  • Extensive cancer-related images.
  • High-quality annotations.
  • Supports clinical research.

  • Focused on biological images.
  • Standardized data formats.
  • Integrates with EBI resources.
ICPSR
  • Extensive health and social science data.
  • Secure data submission with access controls.
  • Comprehensive metadata support.
BioStudies
  • Focused on biological and medical research.
  • Integrates with other EBI resources.
  • Supports a wide range of data types.
IDR
  • Specialized in biological imaging data.
  • Provides rich metadata and experimental context.
  • Supports visualization and reuse of high-content imaging datasets.

White Papers and Protocols

Documenting methodologies and sharing best practices is vital for advancing imaging science. White Papers contribute to scientific knowledge and best practices. Protocols provide detailed methods for replication and validation.
RepositoryAdvantages
arXiv
  • Widely recognized preprint server.
  • Covers multiple disciplines.
  • Fast dissemination of research.
bioRxiv
  • Focused on biological sciences.
  • Enhances visibility within the biology community.
  • Rapid publication process.
protocols.io
  • Specialized for research protocols.
  • Easy version control.
  • Community feedback and sharing.
