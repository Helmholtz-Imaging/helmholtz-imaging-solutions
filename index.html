Helmholtz Imaging Solutions

Solutions are puzzle pieces to successful scientific stories along the imaging pipeline. This includes resources developed and acquired by Helmholtz researchers, but also best practices for utilizing resources from scientific imaging communities worldwide.
  • Developed by the Helmholtz Imaging team and the community
  • Include software, datasets, protocols, and whitepapers
  • Support imaging research across all domains

    How to add a Helmholtz Imaging Solution

    1. Upload your solution resources or collect existing resources.
      • Submit software tools: Have you developed software tools that can be shared?
      • Share datasets: Can your datasets be made available to others?
      • Publish protocols and white papers: Have you published white papers or protocols or any other form of workflow documentation?
    2. Add all solution links to the CONNECT Database.

    Software

    Image Datasets Across Domains

    RepositoryAdvantages
    InfiniteSpace / dCache- Unlimited storage for projects within Helmholtz.
    -Access through Helmholtz ID.
    - Data HTTP - streamable.

    - Collaboration and project management.
    - Promotes open access.
    Open Images Dataset- Millions of annotated images.
    - Rich annotations (bounding boxes, segmentation).
    - Regular updates.
    Figshare- Supports various data types.
    - Easy sharing with privacy options.
    - DOI for citation.
    Zenodo- Free and open access.
    - GitHub integration.
    - Assigns DOIs for datasets.

    White Papers and Protocols

    Documenting methodologies and sharing best practices is vital for advancing imaging science. White Papers contribute to scientific knowledge and best practices. Protocols provide detailed methods for replication and validation.
    RepositoryAdvantages
    arXiv- Widely recognized preprint server.
    - Covers multiple disciplines.
    - Fast dissemination of research.
    bioRxiv- Focused on biological sciences.
    - Enhances visibility within the biology community.
    - Rapid publication process.
    protocols.io- Specialized for research protocols.
    - Easy version control.
    - Community feedback and sharing.

    Related services of the Helmholtz Information and Data Science Platforms

    Researchers working at Helmholtz can contact our sister platforms regarding the following topics:

    • Contact HIFIS for software development questions, for example regarding FAIR principles, code refactoring, or cloud computing.
    • Contact HMC for metadata related questions.
    • Consult Helmholtz AI for AI related method development.
    • Check out the HIDA Course Catalog for data science related trainings.
